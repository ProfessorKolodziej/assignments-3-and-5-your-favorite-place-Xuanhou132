Hyrule

About

Mysterious Land

Hyrule is a mysterious continent. The entire continent is a vast plain in the center, surrounded by deserts, snowy mountains, wetlands and volcanoes. Living on the continent are magical monsters and elf-like people.

Legendary Land

The continent was once ravaged by evil until a princess and brave man stepped forward. Now peace has been restored to the continent, and their bravery has given it much of its history.

Tips

  • Watch out for monsters next to the road.
  • Feel free to ask Hylians for help.
  • Never go to remote mountainous areas where there may be vicious Lynels.
  • Sitting around a campfire at night helps to help you pass the time.
  • Don't try to enter the shrine, it will only waste your travel time.

Transportation

Teleport

Hyrule is a vast continent with many roads in disrepair. For this reason, we do not recommend that visitors use traditional horse-drawn carriages as a means of transportation.

Please use the Teleportation, which allows you to teleport between 120 Temples as long as you carry a Sheikah Slate.

In just a few seconds, you can cross dozens of kilometers.
